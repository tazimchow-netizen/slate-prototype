# Slate Feedback Survey Structure

This survey is designed to be hosted on Typeform (or any modern survey platform) to gather actionable UI/UX and feature feedback from your test users.

## Introduction Screen
**Title:** Help shape the future of Slate!
**Description:** Thanks for testing our early prototype. Your honest feedback will directly influence what we build next. It only takes 2 minutes.
**Button:** Let's go!

## Question 1 (Warm-up / General Sentiment)
**Type:** Rating (1-10)
**Question:** Overall, how would you rate your first impression of Slate?
**Labels:** 1 = Not great, 10 = Amazing

## Question 2 (Core Value Prop)
**Type:** Multiple Choice
**Question:** What were you primarily trying to accomplish while testing Slate today?
- [ ] Brainstorming / Mind mapping
- [ ] Organizing project assets (links, images, tasks)
- [ ] Diagramming / Flowcharts
- [ ] Just exploring the tools
- [ ] Other (Please specify)

## Question 3 (Usability - Pain Points)
**Type:** Long Text
**Question:** What was the *most frustrating* part of using the canvas? 
*(Don't hold back! Did something not work how you expected? Was a tool hard to find or use?)*

## Question 4 (Usability - Highlights)
**Type:** Short Text
**Question:** What was your favorite feature or tool to use?

## Question 5 (Feature Gaps)
**Type:** Multiple Choice (Multiple selection allowed)
**Question:** What features would you need us to add before you would use Slate regularly?
- [ ] File uploads (PDFs, Docs, etc.)
- [ ] Real-time multiplayer collaboration
- [ ] Templates for boards
- [ ] Organizing boards into folders
- [ ] More export options (PDF, PNG)
- [ ] Sticky notes
- [ ] Better styling/formatting options
- [ ] Other (Please specify)

## Question 6 (Product/Market Fit)
**Type:** Multiple Choice
**Question:** How disappointed would you be if you could no longer use Slate?
- [ ] Very disappointed
- [ ] Somewhat disappointed
- [ ] Not disappointed (It isn't really for me)

## Question 7 (Open Ended)
**Type:** Long Text (Optional)
**Question:** Any final thoughts, ideas, or magic wands you’d wave to make Slate perfect?

## Outro Screen
**Title:** Thank you so much!
**Description:** We are carefully reading every piece of feedback to make Slate better. Keep an eye out for updates!
